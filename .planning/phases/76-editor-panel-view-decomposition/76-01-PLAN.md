# Plan 76-01 — Scaffold forms/ + extract start/loop forms

## Goal
Create the form-module scaffold and move the lowest-risk kind-specific form bodies out of `editor-panel-view.ts`.

## Scope

Create:
- `src/views/editor-panel/forms/_shared.ts`
- `src/views/editor-panel/forms/start-form.ts`
- `src/views/editor-panel/forms/loop-form.ts`

Modify:
- `src/views/editor-panel-view.ts`

## Implementation steps

1. Add `_shared.ts` with:
   - `FormContext`
   - `GrowableTextareaOptions` if needed by `renderGrowableTextarea`
   - imports/types only; no runtime coupling back to `EditorPanelView`
2. Add `renderStartForm(container, nodeRecord, ctx)`.
3. Add `renderLoopForm(container, nodeRecord, kind, ctx)` handling:
   - current `loop`
   - legacy informational `loop-start`
   - legacy informational `loop-end`
4. Add `makeFormContext()` to `EditorPanelView`.
5. Keep `buildKindForm` as the test-visible dispatcher method.
6. Replace only `start`, `loop`, `loop-start`, `loop-end` switch-arm bodies with delegations.
7. Preserve old `snippetTreePicker` unmount at the top of `buildKindForm`.

## Non-goals

- Do not extract question/answer/text-block/snippet yet.
- Do not move `renderGrowableTextarea`.
- Do not move canvas-sync code.
- Do not alter tests.
